Abstract

We present CityGenAgent, an agentic pipeline for procedural 3D city generation that unifies layout synthesis, controllable semantics, and multi-scale rendering into a single framework. Given high-level intents or sparse constraints, CityGenAgent decomposes generation into iterative planning, asset-aware placement, and geometry-aware refinement, enabling controllability, diversity, and scalability across city blocks to metropolitan scale.
